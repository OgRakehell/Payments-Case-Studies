## Bill Payment Failure → Product Fix Case Study

### Disclaimer!

This case study is based on a real production scenario. Some technical details, identifiers, volumes, and timelines have been intentionally modified to protect sensitive information while preserving the accuracy of the problem, analysis, and solution.

### Overview

This case study documents the investigation and resolution of recurring electricity bill payment failures for Ikeja Electric (IKEDC) payments routed through Interswitch on digital channels (Mobile App and USSD).

The issue resulted in intermittent transaction failures, delayed confirmations, and increased customer complaints, despite successful debit attempts in some cases. The objective was to identify root causes, reduce failure rates, and improve customer experience and system reliability.

### Product Context

- Product Type: Utility Bill Payments (Electricity)

- Biller: Ikeja Electric (IKEDC)

- Switch / Aggregator: Interswitch

- Channels Affected: Mobile App, USSD

- Payment Flow Type: API-based, near real-time with asynchronous confirmation
